### Project Timeline Notes

- **2008-03-07:** Project declared significant by Coordinator-General
- **2008-09-25:** Commonwealth controlled action designation
- **2009-07-16:** Terms of Reference released
- **2010-02-06 to 2010-03-22:** EIS public consultation period
- **2011-03-14:** Supplementary EIS released
- **2012-01-20:** Coordinator-General's Report published
- **2012-04-19:** Commonwealth Minister approved as controlled action (subject to conditions)
- **2012:** Environmental approvals completed, project halted by LNP Government
- **2015-02:** Queensland Government approved proposal
- **2021-07-03:** Project status changed to "Lapsed"
