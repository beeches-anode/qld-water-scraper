### Key Documents Status

**Detailed Business Case:** Not publicly available as of 2025-12-02. The Bundaberg Burnett Regional Water Assessment (2023) recommended initiating a Detailed Business Case for the Cooranga Weir, and the 2025-26 Queensland Budget allocated $29.7 million for Sunwater to progress planning and design work for the project. However, no completed Detailed Business Case has been published publicly.

**Burnett Water Feasibility Study:** This federally-funded study (completed in phases 2020) identified the Cooranga Weir as a priority project. The study was conducted jointly by North and South Burnett Regional Councils with Jacobs Engineering as the lead consultant. Full study reports have not been located in publicly accessible repositories.
