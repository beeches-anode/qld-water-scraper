### Key Project Details from Research

- **Proposed Location:** North of Crows Nest, on seasonal Emu Creek within the Wivenhoe Dam catchment
- **Estimated Water Yield:** 7.4 gigalitres per year (per 2025 Engeny study)
- **Estimated Cost:** $1.22 billion (includes new dam, three pump stations, two reservoirs, and approximately 45km of trunk raw water pipelines)
- **Study Cost:** Engeny report cost $49,985
- **Current Status:** Project deemed unviable by Toowoomba Regional Council (June 2025); Council instead pursuing Nathan Dam investigation
- **Historical Context:** First detailed water yield examination in almost 30 years since 1996 Department of Natural Resources and Water calculation

**No Detailed Business Case publicly available** - Only pre-feasibility study (Engeny report, May 2025) has been completed. The project has not progressed to detailed business case stage and was ruled out for further investigation in June 2025.
